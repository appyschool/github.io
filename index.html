What's Appy School?

Activities like Student Management, Staff Management, Fee Management, Messaging, Accounting, Attendance, Assessments, Assignments,​ Report Card Generation,​ Time ​T​able, Transportation and many more, can all be performed digitally with Appy School.​

Web console

Management Console for managing all the activities and engagements. Users with privileges can access specific modules in the console. The console can be accessed from anywhere in the web.

Mobile Apps

Mobile Apps for on-the-go communication between the School and Parents. Parents can track their child’s performance, raise concerns, receive instant notifications from the school management

Delivery Channels

With instant notifications, and support over three delivery channels, viz., App notifications, SMS and Emails, Appy School is the one stop solution for any school.​

Modules at a glance

Appy School supports the following key modules which are essential to the functioning of any modern day school.

Student Management

Add/Import/View Student Profiles. Assign standard/section to students and track performance

Staff Management

Maintain catalog of Staff Details including staff profile, standards and subjects assigned, time table, etc.

Fee Management

Define Fee Types and update Fee details of students. Instant Notifications sent to parents on Fee update or Fee Due

Attendance Management

Take/View attendance for students and Staff. Instant Notification triggered on absence. Manage School Calendar

Assignments

Create assignments from Console or Staff App. Parent is notified immediately. Consolidated assignment mail sent daily

Assessments

Provide assessments for students per exam through the Console or Staff mobile App. Generate Report Cards on demand basis for exam(s)

Accounting

Record income, expenses, and other transactions pertaining to school. View credit/debit summary for ledger accounts configured

Transport Management

Add vehicles and bus routes. Map students with vehicles and routes and track the bus in real time

Messaging

Send app notifications, sms or emails to entire school/class, student or a group of students. Messages can also be scheduled

Events & Announcements

Create events and make announcements. This sends automatic notifications to the parent app with option to join the event and book slots

Photo Gallery

Upload pictures of school events from the web console as well as Staff App. The album once shared is avilable for view and download in the parent app

Concerns

Raise concerns and complaints with the school management from the Parennt App. Staff/Management can update the status from the console as well as Staff App
